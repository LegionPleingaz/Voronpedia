# ⚛️ Printers dérivées (Voron-based)

> This section lists printers that **ne sont pas des Voron officiels** mais use their mechanics, kinematics, or design philosophy as a base.

---

## Why derivatives ?

Voron designs are open-source. The community adapts them for :
- **Different formats** (plus petit, plus grand)
- **Additional features** (IDEX, multi-Z, chambre intégrée)
- **Specific uses** (haute température, multi-matière natif)

These projects are not maintained by Voron Design — they have their own community and development pace.
