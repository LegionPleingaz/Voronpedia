# Gantry

> The gantry is the kinematic heart of your Voron. Mods here improve rigidity, precision, or allow wider belts (9mm+) for higher speeds.

---

## Extrusion Backers (Y backers)

> Prevents gantry extrusions from flexing under chamber heat, which causes print artifacts.

| Model     | Compatible |
|-----------|-----------|
| V0 / V0.2 | ❌         |
| Trident   | ✅         |
| V2.4      | ✅         |
| Switchwire| ❌         |

**Difficulty:** 🟢 Easy
**Community status:** ⭐ Proven — strongly recommended for heated chambers

**What you'll need**
- Aluminum or stainless steel backers (machined or printed)
- No printing required if buying a kit

**Links**
- 🔗 [GitHub — VoronUsers / whoppingpochard](https://github.com/VoronDesign/VoronUsers/tree/main/printer_mods/whoppingpochard/extrusion_backers)

**Notes**
> At 60°C+ chamber temps, aluminum extrusions expand and can bow the gantry. Backers (metal inserts slid into the slots) maintain straightness. Near-mandatory mod on V2.4 for high-temp material printing.
