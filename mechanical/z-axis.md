# Z Axis

> The Z axis on Voron V2.4 and Trident uses 4 independent motors (3 on Trident) for automatic leveling (QGL / Z_TILT). Mods here improve precision, repeatability, and rigidity of this axis.

---

## ⚙️ Z Motors

### Galileo 2 Z (G2Z / G2ZXL) ⭐

**Difficulty:** 🟡 Medium
**Community status:** ⭐ Proven

| Model     | Compatible |
|-----------|-----------|
| V2.4      | ✅         |
| Trident   | ✅         |

Replaces stock Z drives with reduction gear motors (Galileo-style). Much more torque, quieter and more precise Z movement. **G2ZXL** is the version for a longer belt.

- 🔗 [GitHub — JaredC01 / Galileo2 Z](https://github.com/JaredC01/Galileo2/tree/main/galileo2_z)

## 🔗 Z Joints

> Z joints connect the Z drives to the bed. Stock uses plastic ball joints. The following mods improve precision and reduce play.

### GE5C ⭐

**Difficulty:** 🟢 Easy
**Community status:** ⭐ Proven — near-standard upgrade

| Model     | Compatible |
|-----------|-----------|
| V2.4      | ✅         |

Replaces stock ball joints with metric GE5C ball bearings (industrial bearings). Eliminates play and improves QGL repeatability.

- 🔗 [GitHub — hartk1213 / Voron2.4_GE5C](https://github.com/hartk1213/MISC/tree/main/Voron%20Mods/Voron%202-Trident/2.4/Voron2.4_GE5C)

---

### RBPB5 ("Gucci GE5C")

**Difficulty:** 🟡 Medium
**Community status:** 🧪 Experimental

Premium replacement with Misumi RBPB5 ball joints. Same principle as GE5C, superior manufacturing quality.

- 🔗 [GitHub — Ramalama2 / Misumi_RBPB5](https://github.com/Ramalama2/Voron-2-Mods/tree/main/Misumi_RBPB5)

---

### Z Ball Joint

**Difficulty:** 🟡 Medium
**Community status:** 🧪 Experimental

Printed + bearing ball joint. Alternative to GE5C if sourcing is difficult.

- 🔗 [GitHub — tanaes / z_ball_joints](https://github.com/tanaes/whopping_Voron_mods/tree/main/z_ball_joints)
