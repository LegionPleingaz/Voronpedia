# Front Idlers

> Front idlers tension the XY belts at the front of the gantry. Mods here replace the stock versions for more rigidity, less play, and better tension.

---

## BFI — Beefy Front Idlers ⭐

**Difficulty:** 🟢 Easy
**Community status:** ⭐ Proven — near-mandatory upgrade

| Model     | Compatible |
|-----------|-----------|
| V2.4      | ✅         |
| Trident   | ✅         |

The reference replacement for stock front idlers. Stiffer, better bearing centering, eliminates flex in the stock idlers. Developed by clee.

- 🔗 [GitHub — clee / VoronBFI](https://github.com/clee/VoronBFI)

**Variant:** [BFI Double Helical Gear Mod](https://www.printables.com/model/1037759-bfi-double-helical-gear-mod-beefy-front-idler) by PAC — version with helical gears to further reduce noise.

> ⭐ Probably one of the first mods to install on a V2.4 or Trident. Stock front idlers are a known weak point.
