# 🧌 Kits

Let's be honest: a big part of the fun comes from hunting down the perfect part, at the right price, tailored to YOUR specific needs. But if you want a kit, here's what the community thinks.

> ⚠️ **Before buying:** always verify the kit targets the exact Voron model you're building (V0.2, Trident, 2.4r2…). Mechanical and electronic configs differ significantly.

---

## 💡 4 rules before buying

1. **Prioritize quality** — a cheap kit often costs more in replacement parts
2. **Check compatibility** — correct model, correct bed size (250/300/350)
3. **Evaluate support** — LDO and Fabreeko have solid reputations for after-sales
4. **Check the Discord** — recent user feedback beats any list

---

## 🏆 Tier list (subjective, community consensus)

### ❌ S Tier — No, never

No perfect kit exists. Every kit has trade-offs, that's the game.

---

### ✅ OK Tier — Recommended

#### LDO Motors ⭐
The **official** supplier recommended by the Voron Design team. The quality reference.

- Superior mechanical parts quality
- Reliable electronics, pre-cut wiring, sometimes pre-assembled
- Serious technical support
- **Availability:** MatterHackers, Fabreeko, KB-3D, EU resellers

#### KB-3D
North American supplier with extensive customization options. Excellent quality and after-sales service.

#### Chaotic Lab
Boutique shop for high-end builds. CNC parts, custom wiring, premium electronics.

#### RatRig
Known for their own CoreXY printers, also compatible with Voron parts.

---

### 🟡 Meh Tier — Decent with caution

#### Formbot
Good budget alternative to LDO. Solid price-to-performance ratio. Some components (wiring) may need adjustment.

#### FYSETC
Known for Voron electronics (Spider board). Budget kits — mechanical quality can vary between batches.

#### Fabreeko
US-based Voron specialist reseller, works with LDO and other reliable manufacturers. Excellent customer service.

---

### 🔴 Sub Tier — For the adventurous

#### Blurolls
Chinese supplier, very competitive pricing. Quality varies by order. Good value for experienced builders.

#### Funssor
Budget kits, wide availability. Some parts may need finishing or upgrading. Plan for QC time on arrival.

#### Siboor
Notably improving in quality and packaging. Better QC than other Sub Tier options. Often includes spare parts.
